Sugar Beam

Sugar Beam is a group of amateur laser-burning enthusiasts who come together to share tips, projects, knowledge, and support in this exciting and developing hobby. We love to see creativity come to life through burns on wood, paper, leather, or even etching on metal. The possibilities are endless. If you can imagine it, design it, then you can burn it.

We're working establishinga community forum here on the site, but in the meantime, we invite you to check out our scheduled classes and zoom meetings found on the "events" page.
